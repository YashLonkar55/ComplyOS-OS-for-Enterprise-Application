# What is Compliance?

Compliance means **following rules, regulations, and policies**.

## Examples

- Customs regulations
- Tax regulations
- Company policies
- Import/export laws
- Financial reporting rules

Companies must prove they're following these rules.

---

# Why is it Difficult?

Compliance information is scattered across multiple sources.

A company may have:

- PDFs
- Emails
- ERP systems
- Excel sheets
- Government portals
- Internal documents

None of these communicate well with each other.

---

# Current Workflow

Imagine a compliance officer on a Monday morning.

They have:

- 200 PDFs
- 40 Excel files
- Government regulation updates
- Email attachments
- ERP reports

Now they need to answer:

> **"Are we compliant?"**

### They manually:

- Read documents
- Compare values
- Search regulations
- Prepare reports
- Review everything again

This process is:

- Slow
- Error-prone
- Time-consuming

---

# Where AI Helps

Instead of reading everything manually:

```text
Upload Documents
        │
        ▼
AI Reads Documents
        │
        ▼
Extracts Information
        │
        ▼
Compares Documents
        │
        ▼
Checks Regulations
        │
        ▼
Highlights Risks
        │
        ▼
Generates Compliance Report
```

> **Important:** AI is helping the human, **not replacing them**.

# Step 2 — What is an Enterprise Compliance Platform?

This is important.

We are **NOT** building another ChatGPT.

Think about enterprise software:

- **ERP (e.g., SAP)** stores business data.
- **CRM (e.g., Salesforce)** stores customer data.

Our platform stores and reasons about **compliance knowledge**.

It becomes the **intelligence layer** for enterprise compliance.

---

# Step 3 — Define Our First Domain

Since we're inspired by **Pax**:

## Version 1 = Trade Compliance

Users upload:

- Commercial Invoice
- Bill of Lading
- Packing List
- Customs Entry
- Export Declaration

The AI will:

- Extract information
- Link related documents
- Find inconsistencies
- Answer regulatory questions
- Generate compliance reports

# Step 4 — What Will We NOT Build?

This is just as important.

We are **not** building:

- ❌ Freight tracking
- ❌ Shipping optimization
- ❌ ERP software
- ❌ Payment gateway
- ❌ Accounting system
- ❌ Warehouse management

We're building the **intelligence layer**, not replacing existing enterprise systems.

# Step 5 — Success Criteria

When **Version 1 (V1)** is complete, a user should be able to:

1. Create an organization.
2. Upload trade-related documents.
3. Have AI extract structured information.
4. Review extracted data.
5. Ask compliance questions.
6. Receive answers with supporting evidence.
7. Generate a compliance report.
